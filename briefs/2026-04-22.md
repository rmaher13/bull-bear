BIG THREE

- Apple named its new CEO yesterday and the market is still chewing on it. ONE THING WORTH UNDERSTANDING

GE Aerospace's thing today is called "buy the rumor, sell the news," and it happens constantly. Here's the idea: when a stock has been climbing for weeks before earnings, that means investors already priced in a great report. So when the great report actually arrives, there's nobody left to buy. The people who were waiting to get in already got in. Now they're taking their profits and leaving. The stock drops even though the news is good. It's like a bar that's been hyped for months finally opening — first night's packed, second night's empty. The fundamentals were fine. The expectations were just already baked in.
